The field of language is as diverse as there are cultures. We can observe the evolution of language throughout the years. Beyond just learning language, in this course we understand the nuance of its development through the years. The use of language throughout history and the major events that caused many languages to either change, evolve, develop or disappear. The study of language isn't just learning the various languages, it's understanding them.

Modern language courses lean towards the study of language today and how it was developed. It leans on the new words and slang that are added monthly. They study how languages adapt to the needs of modern culture. It compensates for the newer emotions and experiences we feel in this era. Modern language now focusses on studying the trends in the development of new words and phrases and the reasons for their conceptualization or creation.
