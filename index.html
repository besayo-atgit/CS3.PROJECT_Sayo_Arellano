What is Tusok for Thought?

Tusok for Thought (T4T) is a project that highlights the valuability of information and fun facts in real life. As people who really enjoy placing too much time in researching random rabbit holes that we find interesting, T4T became more than just a CS3 project and became rather a personal and passion project. Our mission is to create a website that gives tidbits of info in every person's hands so that they can find their interests and niches.

This is the primary foundation and principle pf T4T: niches. We help find your niches so that you can seek new hobbies, passions, and interests sparked by fun facts!
